About Us

Welcome to Velvet Ember, where culinary excellence meets warm hospitality. Established in 2023, our restaurant is dedicated to providing an unforgettable dining experience with a menu inspired by diverse global flavors. From authentic Italian pastas to exquisite Japanese sushi, each dish is crafted with love and the finest ingredients.

Our team of passionate chefs and friendly staff are committed to creating a welcoming atmosphere for every guest. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or enjoying a casual meal, we strive to make every visit memorable.
